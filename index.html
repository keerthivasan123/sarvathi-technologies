Hello, We are Sravathi Tech.

About Us

Sravathi Advance Process Technologies Private Limited was incorporated in Bengaluru in July 2019 by three founders each having more than two decades of global experience and expertise gained from world reputed multinational companies. The company is MSME, which provides advanced differentiated process technologies, research and development support for Sravathi’s businesses. Sravathi is located in the Industrial Town, Rajajinagar in Bengaluru with a built-up facility of about 15000 sq.ft and can embrace strength of 100 employees. Currently the organization has power house of 50+ researchers of multi-disciplinary competencies with abundant scope for expansion.

MISSION STATEMENT

Sravathi’s aim is to contribute to the enhancement of quality of life through creation of innovative advanced technologies using state-of-art techniques such as Artificial Intelligence and Process Intensification and through the provision of pharmaceuticals and other chemicals addressing diverse needs of people.

VISION STATEMENT

Global Advanced process Innovator with Competitive Advantage in Pharmaceuticals, fine chemicals, high value chemicals, petrochemicals, agro chemicals, flavours and fragrances.

Our Founders

Dr. KISHAN GURRAM

Managing Director and CEO of the company. Doctorate in Catalysis from Indian Institute of Chemical Technology (IICT), Hyderabad, India and had multiple post-doctoral stints at Europe & South Korea. He has more than two decades of industrial experience in multinational organizations, GE and SABIC. He has held various global executive roles in R&D, manufacturing, business, site operations in India, Europe, KSA. He has strong technical expertise is in the area of catalysis, monomers, polymers and has translated multiple technologies from lab scale concept stage to Kilo tonnes commercialization scale in plants. He has built excellent network of toll manufacturers in India and translated 20+ technologies to plants through toll manufacturing to the plants located in different geographies. He is member of various professional bodies in Indian industries, premier academic institutions in India, editorial board of various peer reviewed international reputed journals. He has 50+ peer reviewed journal publications and 50+ international patents filed most of them being commercially practised in plants and is recipient of various top-notch awards in the companies. He is certified Green belt in Six Sigma quality tools in GE.

Dr. SIVAKUMAR S

Sivakumar S is the Vice President for the Process, obtained Doctorate in Chemical Engineering in 1995 from Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru. He has held various senior roles with more than two and half decades of industrial experience in Indian & Multinational organizations, GE and SABIC. He has strong expertise in the area of Chemical Process Development, Reaction engineering, Design and operation of multi-purpose pilot plant and building state of art process intensification lab. In a career of 20yrs, he has successfully translated multiple technologies (process and products) from concept stage to commercialization scale in plants in India, USA and Spain. He has strong expertise in process development, Conceptual Process Design (CEP), process costing, process modelling, and process translations. He has 6 awarded patents and 15+ internationally filed patents in product and process. He is certified DFSS Black Belt in GE.

Dr. G.V. RAMANARAYANAN

Ramanarayanan GV is the Vice President for the Chemistry, obtained Doctorate in Organic Chemistry from Institute of Chemical Technology, Mumbai, India (Formerly UDCT). He has held various senior roles in organization with more than two decades of industrial experience in multinational organizations, GE and SABIC. He has strong expertise in the area of synthetic organic chemistry, catalysis, bulk drug synthesis, asymmetric synthesis, organic transformations, monomers, polymers, pharmaceuticals, specialities, fine chemicals and petrochemicals. He is certified Green belt in Six Sigma quality tools in GE. He has translated multiple technologies from lab scale concept stage to Kilo tonnes commercialization scale in local and global plants. He has excellent experience in toll manufacturing and translated 20+ technologies to plants and supplied the speciality chemicals/monomers / polymers located in different geographies. He has 60+ international patents filed most of them are being practised in commercial scale in plants and he is recipient of various awards in the company.
